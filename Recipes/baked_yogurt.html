Baked Yogurt

Tastes like healthy custard, made with 6 ingredients and contains 8 grams of protein!

This baked yogurt is like a healthy custard or flan. You can make it with regular yogurt or as a good tip use greek yogurt or skyr for added protein!!

Home

Ingredients for Baked Yogurt

  • 2 Cups Greek yogurt or Skyr
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1/4 Cup maple syrup or honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 TBSP cornstarch
  • 1 1/2 Cups berries, divided

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F and line 8X8 square baking dish with parchment paper. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, add yogurt, eggs, maple syrup, and vanilla. Whisk until smooth. Add cornstarch and whisk again until well combined.
  3. Add 1 Cup of berries and give a gentle stir. Then pour mixture into previously prepared baking dish and sprinkle remaining 1/2 Cup berries on top.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes or until yogurt in the center is set and not juggly anymore. Remove from the oven and let cool almost completely.
  5. Cut into 9 slices and enjoy as is or with more maple syrup!

Notes

  • Store: Refrigerate in an airtight container for up to 5 days.
  • Freeze: Freeze in an airtight container for up to 3 months. Thaw on a counter for a few hours or in the fridge overnight.
