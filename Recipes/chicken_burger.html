Greek Chicken Burger

An origianl beef or lamb recipe transformed into a healthier chicken version.

The original recipe called for lamb or beef, but we have substituted for ground chicken instead to decrease the fat. The sauce easily doubles as a flavorful dip for veggies or chips.

Ingredients for Greek Chicken Burger

Sauce:
  • 1/3 Cup fat-free plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 Cup chopped peeled cucumber
  • 1/4 Cup crumbled reduced-fat feta cheese
  • 1 1/2 tsp snipped fresh dill
  • 1 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 small garlic clove
Burgers:
  • 1medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 Cup dried bread crumbs
  • 1 TBSP dried oregano
  • 1 TBSP lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 4 hamburger buns, split
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 tomato slices

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix the sauce ingredients. Refrigerate until serving
  2. In a large bowl, combine the first 7 burger ingredients. Add chicken; mix lightly but thoroughly. Shape into four 1/2 in. thick patties.
  3. On a lightly greased grill over medium heat, grill burgers, covered, or broil 4 in. from heat 5-7 minutes on each side or until thermometer reads 165 degrees F. Serve on buns with lettuce, tomato, and sauce
